Background

Karama Computer Services Co.( KCSC) has been established in 1991 in Jordan to be a member of the KCSC group of companies that have been functioning in Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, and other Arab countries since 1977.

KCSC is well experienced to offer the total consultancy approach for turnkey solutions. This approach will cover; the application software, the consultancy, the training, and all other services required providing a total comprehensive solution, which handles all the needs of the customer in all required application areas.
